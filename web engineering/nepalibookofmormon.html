The "Nepali Book of Mormon" is a tool to read scripture for all the nepalese people around the world. This website: "Nepali Book of mormon" is expected to solve the issues that new generation face while reading. Most of the nepali later day saints population stay outside Nepal. And the youngester in and outside nepal have hard time reading Nepali and understanding english. People are at that point where they speak four languages in a day but they are not perfect in a single. Through this sites, we hope this problem can be minimized.

This is just a prototype. The final version will come soon.
